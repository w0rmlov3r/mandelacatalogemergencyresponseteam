Learn More About Alternates

What Are Alternates?

Alternates are not merely creatures—they are manifestations of a terrifying, all-consuming force. These entities prey on humanity, lurking in plain sight, mimicking our loved ones, and using psychological manipulation to induce paranoia and fear. Their goal is to destabilize and ultimately eradicate human existence.

The Nature of an Alternate

Alternates are beings that transcend the normal rules of existence. They cannot be explained by science or reason. Instead, their existence is rooted in a terrifyingly alien logic, one that allows them to manipulate human emotions, thoughts, and actions. Here’s what makes them so deadly:

  • They can appear as anyone: Alternates can perfectly mimic people you've known your entire life.
  • They use fear as a weapon: Every encounter is designed to manipulate you emotionally and mentally.
  • They spread through suggestion: Their influence grows like a virus, infecting the mind with fear and despair.

Key Fact: The Silent Influence of an Alternate

While the physical appearance of an Alternate may seem normal, the influence they exert is psychological. Their presence can distort your sense of reality, slowly pushing you towards desperation and self-harm.

How Do They Manipulate You?

Alternates have a terrifying ability to manipulate their victims. Their strategies involve a mix of subtle and overt tactics:

  • Gaslighting: They make you question your own reality, causing confusion and doubt.
  • Psychological warfare: They provoke irrational thoughts, feeding into your deepest insecurities.
  • Emotional blackmail: Alternates often manipulate feelings of guilt, fear, and love to control their victims.

Warning: Trust No One

As the Alternate spreads its influence, it becomes harder to trust even those closest to you. The more you try to resist, the stronger their psychological hold becomes. Stay alert and keep your distance.

What to Do If You Encounter an Alternate

If you suspect an encounter with an Alternate, your safety is paramount. Here are immediate actions you should take:

  • Do not engage: Avoid any confrontation or communication.
  • Stay calm: Your mind must remain clear to make logical decisions.
  • Seek help: Alert a trusted person and move quickly to a safe place.
  • Preserve your thoughts: Keep a clear mental record of events to avoid manipulation.
