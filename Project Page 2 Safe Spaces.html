URGENT ALERT!

IF YOU ENCOUNTER AN ALTERNATE ENTITY, DO NOT ENGAGE OR TRY TO COMMUNICATE. QUICKLY RETREAT TO THE NEAREST SAFE SPACE.

If you are in immediate danger, here are some safe spaces you should head towards:

  • Underground bunkers (preferably far from population centers)
  • Remote, isolated cabins in forested areas
  • Emergency shelters (check your local listings for secure locations)
  • Public spaces with high foot traffic and security cameras (shopping malls, large stores)
  • Abandoned underground metro stations (if accessible)
