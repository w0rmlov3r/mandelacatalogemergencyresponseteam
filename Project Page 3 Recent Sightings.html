Recent Sightings

Recent sightings of Alternates have been reported in the following locations. Keep your guard up, and avoid areas where these sightings have occurred. The presence of an Alternate may indicate a dangerous situation.

Reported Sightings:

  • New York City - 5th Avenue: A figure was seen walking among the crowd at night. Witnesses described it as having an unnaturally still presence.
  • Los Angeles - Downtown Area: Several people reported hearing whispering voices in an abandoned building. The sounds were traced to no one in particular.
  • Chicago - South Side: Surveillance footage shows a dark figure lurking near the alleyway behind a convenience store. No other individuals were nearby during the sighting.

Important: Avoid Confrontation

If you encounter a person in one of these areas displaying strange behavior or a distorted presence, do not attempt to confront them. Stay as far away as possible and alert authorities immediately.

Stay Safe

Stay vigilant, and keep in mind the warning signs of an Alternate encounter. If you notice any of the following, immediately retreat to a safe space:

  • Unnatural stillness or awkward movement patterns
  • People that seem to mimic your movements or gestures
  • Strange or unsettling facial expressions
